Offgrid Kit
===========

1) Verify integrity (recommended):
   ./scripts/verify_checksums.sh

2) Open the launcher:
   Windows: double-click START_WINDOWS.bat
   Linux:   ./START_LINUX.sh
   macOS:   ./START_MAC.command

3) If services are already running, open dashboard:
   http://<HOST_IP>:8090

The dashboard auto-loads all discovered ZIM files.
Use the launcher or dashboard buttons to open knowledge, maps, AI, ebooks, updates, and setup checks.
