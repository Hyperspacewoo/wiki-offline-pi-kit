# Content Takedown / Correction Policy

If you believe distributed content infringes rights, lacks required attribution, or contains dangerous inaccuracies, submit a report to:

- **Contact:** <maintainer-email>
- **Subject line:** "Offline Kit Takedown/Correction Request"

## Include in your request

1. Exact file/content reference (path, article, screenshot if possible)
2. Nature of issue (copyright/license/attribution/safety/error)
3. Basis for request and evidence links
4. Preferred resolution (remove, correct attribution, update warning, etc.)

## Handling targets

- Acknowledge within 3 business days
- Provide initial decision/status within 10 business days
- Apply urgent safety mitigations as quickly as practical

## Non-legal notice

This process is a project policy for triage/risk reduction and does not create legal obligations beyond applicable law.
