# Starter Content Pack (Recommended)

For paid-ready value, include at least one high-quality resource in each category:

- prepping/
- medical/
- comms/
- navigation/

Place ebooks under `ebooks/<category>/`.

Suggested minimum bundle:
- 1 general prep handbook
- 1 wilderness first aid guide
- 1 radio communications quick guide
- 1 land nav/topographic guide
