Example copy commands from your current machine:

1) Copy kit folder:
scp -r /home/void/.openclaw/workspace/pi-wiki-kit pi@<PI_IP>:~/

2) Copy ZIM file (large; may take a while):
scp /home/void/wiki/zim/wikipedia_en_all_nopic_2025-12.zim pi@<PI_IP>:/mnt/wiki-ssd/

Then SSH into Pi:
ssh pi@<PI_IP>
cd ~/pi-wiki-kit
chmod +x install_pi_wiki.sh setup_kiwix_service.sh
./install_pi_wiki.sh
./setup_kiwix_service.sh /mnt/wiki-ssd/wikipedia_en_all_nopic_2025-12.zim
